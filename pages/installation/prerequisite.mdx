# Prerequisite
Certain elements, regardless of your operating system, are imperative.

## Discord application
1. First, we must go to the developer section of Discord to create a [new application](https://discord.com/developers/applications).
<Callout emoji="⚠️">
    If you were not logged in, and when you do, you will be redirected
    to the chat panel, the usual place where you chat.
    That's not where we have to go though, we, this is where we have to go
    [`https://discord.com/developers/applications`](https://discord.com/developers/applications)
</Callout>


![Landscape](/installation/prerequisite/createApp.png)

2. At the top right, click on the **"New Application"** button

![Landscape](/installation/prerequisite/createApp2.png)

![Landscape](/installation/prerequisite/createApp3.png)

3. Once the application is created, go to the "Bot" section, click on "Add Bot", then confirm "Yes, Do It!"

![Landscape](/installation/prerequisite/createApp4.png)

4. Scroll down to the "Privileged Gateway Intents" — Be sure to enable the `Presence Intent` and `Server Members Intent`, then save.

<Callout emoji="❗">
    If your Luna instance is in more than 100 guilds, it will need to be [verified](https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040720412),
    this procedure will not be detailed in this guide
</Callout>
